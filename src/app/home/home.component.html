About Salesforce

Salesforce is a leading customer relationship management (CRM) platform that helps businesses manage their sales, marketing, and customer support activities. It offers a wide range of tools and services to streamline business processes and enhance customer relationships.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Strong Experience with Salesforce Lightning components Frameworks (Aura, LWC)
  • Experience in Salesforce Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Experience Cloud.
  • Strong Experience with Configuration, Customization, Programming with APIs, Apex triggers, Batches, Flows, and implementing new instances of Salesforce from scratch and deploying processes.
  • Must have knowledge of front-end development.
  • Apex, Apex API, trigger, data loader, Visualforce, Chatter, Projects in Salesforce, Design and implements Salesforce integration using REST And SOAP, API integration with Third-Party Systems, Ant Migration Tools, Salesforce Lightning, Force.com, Aura, SOQL, Async and Sync Apex, different tools knowledge (Bitbucket, Azure DevOps), Copado ANT, Workbench, CPQ, Tableau, Changeset.

Salesforce Ecosystem

The Salesforce ecosystem comprises various products and platforms, including Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud, Commerce Cloud, and more. Each component is designed to address specific business needs and can be customized to suit your organization's requirements.
